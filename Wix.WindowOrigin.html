Wix.WindowOrigin

Wix.WindowOrigin

Represents a Wix popup window origin. A window can be positioned where it is origin is the view port (0,0) or where the origin is another widget (x,y).

Absolute position. The popup will be placed relative to a given x,y coordinates that their origin is the top-left corner of the widget.
